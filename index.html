Basic:

Welcome to stone incremental, in this game you try to make as much stone as possible!

Stone:

This is the main resource of the game, it also can get you powerful boosts later on

Magma:

This is the first reset, it boosts stone and is needed later on

Compressed Stone:

This is the first partial reset in the game, as it doesn't reset magma

Mine Tier:

This is the second reset layer, and it gives incredible boosts

Lava:

This is an entirely automated currency, that boosts stone and magma, it also automates magma gain
